Precise draft surveyor
ensuring maritime cargo compliance.

Cultiver

As a seasoned draft surveyor, my role is to meticulously assess and verify the weight, quantity, and quality of cargo on vessels, ensuring compliance with international standards and regulations. Employing a keen eye for detail, I employ cutting-edge measurement techniques, modern technology, and my vast expertise to accurately determine cargo volumes, assess trim and stability, and detect any discrepancies or damages. My work is pivotal in preventing disputes, ensuring safety, and facilitating smooth cargo operations for the maritime industry. With my unwavering commitment to precision and my ability to deliver comprehensive reports, I provide invaluable support to maritime stakeholders, fostering trust and efficiency in global trade.
